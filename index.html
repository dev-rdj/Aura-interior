About Us

At Aura Interiors, we specialize in crafting high-quality modular interiors that combine functionality with timeless design. With over a decade of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for delivering personalized interior solutions that reflect the unique tastes and lifestyles of our clients. All our modular furniture is manufactured in-house at our state-of-the-art factory located on Sarjapura Road, ensuring complete quality control and precision at every stage. We use only premium materials and the latest technology to create interiors that are not only beautiful but also built to last. As a testament to our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we offer a 10-year warranty on all our materials.
